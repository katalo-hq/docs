Katalo is a server-to-server API that generates AI-staged real estate imagery. Your backend submits one source image, creates a generation job, and receives signed output URLs — delivered by webhook or available to poll. This documentation covers everything you need to integrate Katalo into your platform: authentication, the generation lifecycle, webhook delivery, operational limits, and billing.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Ingest the source image">
    Call `POST /api/v1/source-assets` with a `source_url` or multipart file upload. Poll `GET /api/v1/source-assets/{ingest_id}` until a `source_asset_id` is returned.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a generation job">
    Call `POST /api/v1/generations` with your `source_asset_id` and workflow options such as room type, style preset, and capture type.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for completion">
    Receive a signed webhook callback when the job completes, or poll `GET /api/v1/generations/{job_id}` until it reaches `succeeded` or `failed`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use the outputs">
    Read the signed output URLs from the response. Copy assets into your own storage if you need retention beyond the URL expiry window.
  </Step>
</Steps>
